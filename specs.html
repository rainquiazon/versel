DLSS & AI Advanced Tech

The Gigabyte AORUS MASTER 18 BZH is equipped with NVIDIA DLSS 4.0, which uses advanced AI upscaling to deliver higher frame rates without sacrificing image quality. This allows gamers to enjoy ultra-smooth gameplay even in demanding AAA titles. In addition, the laptop features AI-powered noise reduction to enhance audio clarity during in-game communication and media playback. Its AI-assisted thermal optimization intelligently adjusts fan speeds and system performance to keep temperatures low, ensuring consistent performance under heavy workloads. Together, these technologies provide a cutting-edge gaming experience that blends speed, precision, and intelligent system management.

Materials

The laptop chassis is crafted from a magnesium-aluminum alloy, providing a perfect balance between durability and lightweight portability. Reinforced carbon fiber vents are integrated to optimize cooling efficiency while maintaining structural strength. The keyboard is finished with premium RGB materials, offering both durability and an appealing aesthetic for gamers. Overall, the combination of high-quality materials ensures the AORUS MASTER 18 BZH is not only powerful under the hood but also visually sleek and robust for everyday use.
